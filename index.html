  • Our Team
    An enduring friendship and the desire for a new adventure is the story
    behind La Vita Hair Design.
    Angela Durante and Cinzia Ruatta share a common passion they love
    their craft!
    Angela began hairdressing at the age of 17 and opened her first
    salon in 1993 in Turin, Italy.
    Angela continued opening salons and eventually owned 6 with
    over 30 employees.
    Cinzia became part of the industry as a 15 year old.
    Cinzia’s hunger for knowledge and love for her craft enabled her to gain
    a high level of experience, which in 1993 led her to become an employee
    of internationally renowned franchise “Jean Louis David”.
    Cinzia went on to become one of Jean Louis David’s leading educators.
    After 4 years Cinzia also opened her own salon in the city of Asti, Italy.
    Angela and Cinzia met in Italy as a result of salon ownership and education;
    their friendship grew and so did their dream to experience Australia.
    La Vita Hair Design is a dynamic blend of Italian passion,
    experience and love of the craft.
